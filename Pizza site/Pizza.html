Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that consists of a round, flat base of dough baked with a variety of toppings, most commonly including tomato sauce, cheese, and a selection of meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is a versatile and customizable dish, enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sause
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Basil
  • salt
  • Toppings of choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)

recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza for added flavor.
  7. Add minced garlic, oregano, basil, and a pinch of salt.
  8. Place your desired toppings on the pizza.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy!
